About Us

At STEEM, we take immense pride in our team of highly qualified and experienced tutors. Many of our tutors are First-Class graduates from prestigious universities. Our team members have excelled academically, achieving top grades in their A-Levels, and have accumulated extensive professional experience in sectors such as hedge funds, consulting and fintech startups.

The foundation of our success lies in the high-quality tuition from our tutors. At STEEM, we are committed to giving back and ensuring that students receive the opportunities for success. Our tutors are passionate about supporting students in their academic journeys, providing expert guidance and personalised teaching to help them excel in their studies and achieve their goals.

Experience

Over the past five years, our tutors have delivered over 5000 hours of lessons, spanning GCSE, A-Level, and 11+ exam preparation. Our expertise in online classrooms ensures students maximise their learning experience.

We meticulously tailor our approach to each student's exam board, diving into specifications and mark schemes to deliver content and techniques that meet their specific requirements.

Our lessons are carefully planned yet adaptable, focusing on skill development, clear explanations, and ample practice to ensure students excel.
